refers to cadastral entityop back to ToC or Object Property ToC

IRI: http://vocab.linkeddata.es/datosabiertos/def/oferta-inmobiliaria/refersToCadastralEntity

Relationship linking a real estate listing to its corresponding cadastral entity (parcel or property). This allows access to cadastral information such as use, class, address, and reference through the Cadastre ontology. For land-only sales, it links to a CadastralParcel. For buildings or units, it links to a CadastralProperty.
